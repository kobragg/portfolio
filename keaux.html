that's enough about my name. here's more about what i do:


since January, i’ve been a reporter at Reveal from the Center for Investigative Reporting working on two Mississippi-based projects.

in July 2019, i joined FRONTLINE as the lead reporter for an investigative documentary about civil rights cold cases .

my first "big girl" job was at an alt-weekly in Jackson, Miss. i covered criminal justice issues with a focus on kids charged as adults and policing. the capital city's mayor and police department changed several city policies as a result of this reporting .

i also teach writing at a kids unit inside an adult prison. everyone in my course has been sentenced as an adult. .

you can read other freelance work i've done here at Scalawag Magazine and The Appeal.
